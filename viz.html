Welcome to VIZbeta, Ohio State's online requirements visualizer.

You can use VIZbeta to get a quick idea of how broadly your courses will apply across the majors at OSU.

Search by selecting from the drop-down menus in the search pane.

Hide/reveal the Search Pane by clicking "Collapse/Expand Search".

VIZbeta will display your results:

✓ means the item is approved according to your search.
✘ means it is not approved.

You can search either by Courses or Programs.

The Courses search displays all majors that can accept all selected courses as fulfilling a requirement (other than Open Options or electives).

The Programs search displays all courses that can fulfill requirements for all selected majors.

To switch between searches, click the "Search By ..." button in the Search Pane.

VIZbeta does not fully represent all requirements or replace in-person advising. Consult with an advisor before scheduling.
